Character Illustrations and Images of Taro Sakamoto.

Sakamoto's overweight appearance after retiring.

Sakamoto is a tall, fat man with slicked back gray hair that is tied into a ponytail at the back of his head (similar to a man bun) and he has a small broom mustache. He typically wears an apron over a T-shirt and long pants. Sakamoto prefers to wear slippers, even when outside. His eyes are usually hidden by the glare of his round glasses.

Sakamoto during his days as an assassin

In the past, Sakamoto was much leaner with no mustache. His facial features were more defined and his eyes were visible through his glasses. Through burning through his accumulated calories, Sakamoto can revert to this form for a power boost. Shin notes his transformed appearance makes him look handsome.

Aoi Sakamoto (坂本 葵, Sakamoto Aoi)

is a character in the Sakamoto Days series. She is the wife of Taro Sakamoto and the mother of Hana Sakamoto. She was the reason why Sakamoto abandoned his life as an assassin and showed him a better path to protecting his loved ones.

Hana Sakamoto (坂本 花, Sakamoto Hana)

is the daughter of Taro Sakamoto and Aoi Sakamoto.

Shin Asakura (朝倉あさくらシン Asakura Shin?)

Also known as the Clairvoyant 千里眼の, is the deuteragonist in the series Sakamoto Days. Shin is an ex-hitman and an employee at the Sakamoto's Store that was once an old partner and former student of Taro Sakamoto. After failing to kill Sakamoto, Shin joins him and his family.

Lu Shaotang (陸ルー 少糖シャオタン Rū Shaotan, Lu Xiaotang by Viz)

is a major supporting character in the Sakamoto Days series. She is the daughter of a Triad Family. She currently works alongside Shin as a store clerk at Sakamoto's and can be considered a part of the Sakamoto Family.

Yoichi Nagumo (永沼よういち Nagumo Yōichi)

is a character in the Sakamoto Days series. He is a former assassin who worked for the Order and a former classmate of Taro Sakamoto. He is currently working as a part-time worker at Sakamoto's Store.

Heisuke Mashimo (真島へいすけ Mashimo Heisuke)

is a character in the Sakamoto Days series. He is an ex-hitman who worked for the Order and a former classmate of Taro Sakamoto. He is currently working as a part-time worker at Sakamoto's Store.

Rion Akao (赤尾リオン Akao Rion)

is a character in the Sakamoto Days series. She is a former assassin who worked for the Order and a former classmate of Taro Sakamoto. She is currently working as a part-time worker at Sakamoto's Store.
