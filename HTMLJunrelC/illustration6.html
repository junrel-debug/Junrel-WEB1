Lu Shaotang (陸ルー 少糖シャオタン Rū Shaotan, Lu Xiaotang by Viz)

is a major supporting character in the Sakamoto Days series. She is the daughter of a Triad Family. She currently works alongside Shin as a store clerk at Sakamoto's and can be considered a part of the Sakamoto Family.
